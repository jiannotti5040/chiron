Paste a sequence. Watch it prove — or refuse. The demo core runs in your browser: real Python, real refusals, no server.

What this page runs: prototype/browser_core.py — the repository's public demo core, fetched verbatim and executed by CPython-in-WebAssembly. It shares the licensed engine's contract — exact arithmetic, a stamp only when the recovered rule predicts held-out terms exactly, refusal otherwise — and is strictly weaker: fewer hypothesis families, a more conservative evidence rule. It is not the licensed engine. The licensed engine's frozen outputs, graded against oeis.org, are in eval/.
