# Commercial access

Use Chiron commercially when you have a supported, structured output that needs a repeatable exact-check gate—not a generic promise that every AI answer can be certified. A paid license grants **commercial-use rights** and access to **`chiron-vault`**: the full engine as one self-contained deterministic file, with the right to run, modify, extend, and contribute back.

Before buying, challenge the public product on non-sensitive test data: **[check a supported claim live](https://jiannotti5040.github.io/chiron/playground/#claim-checker)**, **[run the published external eval](eval/README.md)**, or **[inspect the source-available proof layer](README.md)**. Commercial access is for using the full engine in an organization or revenue-generating workflow.

Every tier includes the same core engine. Higher tiers add seats, deployment scope, and the explicitly listed onboarding or support provisions. Start at the smallest scope that matches your use.

## What every license includes

- **Commercial-use rights** to the full Chiron engine.
- **`chiron-vault` access** — the complete folded monolith (72+ modules), the
  certification brain, the composer, and the operator dashboard, offline and
  self-contained.
- **The right to modify and extend** the engine for your own use.
- **A contribution channel** back into the ecosystem. Improvements to the
  certified core are owner-reviewed so the verification property is preserved —
  the honesty of the `verified` stamp is what you are paying to protect.

## What it does not include unless stated

Individual and Team are self-serve. Business includes an onboarding call; Enterprise includes the support listed above. No warranty of results is made for your own data—the verification claims refer to the engine’s published, reproducible self-test gates. All sales are final; cancel anytime and commercial rights run to the end of the paid period.
