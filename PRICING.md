## What every license includes

- **Commercial-use rights** to the full Chiron engine.
- **`chiron-vault` access** — the complete folded monolith (72+ modules), the
  certification brain, the composer, and the operator dashboard, offline and
  self-contained.
- **The right to modify and extend** the engine for your own use.
- **A contribution channel** back into the ecosystem. Improvements to the
  certified core are owner-reviewed so the verification property is preserved —
  the honesty of the `verified` stamp is what you are paying to protect.

## What it does not include unless stated

Individual and Team use self-serve checkout. Business includes an onboarding call; Enterprise includes the support listed above. No warranty of results is made for your own data—the verification claims refer to the engine’s published, reproducible self-test gates. All sales are final; cancel anytime and commercial rights run to the end of the paid period.

3. Reply from the checkout email and publish the one-time public-Gist verification requested by the owner. That confirms control of the requested GitHub account before a private-repository invitation is granted. Team and Business seat setup is handled with you after purchase.
